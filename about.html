The Story Behind
The Lens

Frame 2 Remember was born out of a passion for capturing the raw, unscripted beauty of life. Established in 2018, we have been dedicated to preserving memories across New Zealand.

Founded by Rohit Basak, our mission has always been simple: to create timeless images that tell a story. Whether it's the intimate glance during a wedding or the dynamic energy of a corporate event, we approach every shoot with the same level of care and artistic vision.

Based in the stunning landscapes of New Zealand, we draw inspiration from our surroundings—incorporating natural light and authentic moments to create photography that resonates for a lifetime.
